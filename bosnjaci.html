ABOUT

The company Dinoza d.o.o. (Ltd.) Bošnjaci was found in 2009 as a daughter-company whose 90% owner is Dinoza d.o.o. (Ltd.) Orašje. The knowledge and experience that Dinoza d.o.o. Orašje has gained through the several decades of business greatly contributed to the growth and development of Dinoza d.o.o. (Ltd.) Bošnjaci. On the other hand, Dinoza d.o.o. (Ltd.) Bošnjaci has a strategically important position in Croatia as a EU member. It significantly facilitates the administrative side of the business with other EU member states. The company started in 2015 and positioned itself as a 90% exporter of domestic salted raw skin (simmental) which it successfully places on the foreign market, such as neighbouring countries as well as other EU members. As its long- term goal, the company is planning to start cooperating with countries of other continents, too.
